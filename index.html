FFC Manual
Welcome to Final Fantastic Card!

Gameplay

At the beginning of the game, each player starts with 7 cards. The aim of the game is to clear all your cards before every other player.

To view your hand, use the hand command.

To play a card, use the play command.

More commands can be found in the Commands section below.

Cards

When it is your turn to play a card, you may only play:

  • Cards of the same color;
  • Cards of the same number or symbol;
  • Wildcards.

If you cannot play any of these cards from your hand, you must draw from the deck using the draw command.

Card format

The first character of the card may be one of R, G, B, Y or +. RGBY represent their respective colors (red, green, yellow or blue). + indicates a wildcard.

If the first character indicates a color, then the second character indicates its face value. It may be a number from 0 to 9, S representing skip, R indicating reverse, or + indicating draw 2.

Otherwise, when the first character is +, the second character indicates whether the card is a normal wildcard (+W), or a Wild Draw 4 (+4). Additionally, if you choose to play one of these cards, you must specify a color with the respective color character, such as +4R or +WY

Card effects

Color/number cards have no special effect, and play continues as normal. S (skip) cards cause the player next in the playing order (see status) to be skipped. R (reverse) cause the direction of play to be reversed. In 2 player games, both S and R cards have the same effect and repeat your turn. A + card makes the next player draw 2 extra cards from the deck, except when the cumulative draw house rule is enabled (see below).

Wildcards change the color of play. Indicate the color you want as explained above. Additionally, Wild Draw 4 cards (+4) make the next player draw 4 extra cards. However, you may only play them if you have no other cards to play. The next player may challenge you on this decision. If challenged and you did play it legally, they have to draw 6 cards instead. However, if your play was illegal, you must draw 4 cards.

Commands

When you switch games, you must prefix the command with the respective game ID. Otherwise, the bot will remember the last game you chose (and will automatically switch you to any newly started game).

  • hand: view your hand;
  • play [c]: play a card c;
  • draw: draw a card from the deck;
  • pass: after drawing a card, you may pass your turn instead of playing;
  • status: show the status of other players;
  • chat [msg]: broadcasts a chat message to other players;
  • !: challenges another player;
  • nudge: reminds the current player it is their turn;
  • ffc: call out FFC to indicate you only have 1 card left;
  • drop: leave the room. If spectating, stop spectating;
  • spectate: leave the game and start spectating.

An unrecognised command will be sent as a chat message directly.

House rules

In the initial ffc:join command, you may add letters to specify house rules, such as ffc:join c:

  • c: cumulative draw, where consecutive plays of Draw 2 cards accumulate cards until someone cannot play a Draw 2 card. Then, that player draws all accumulated cards.
